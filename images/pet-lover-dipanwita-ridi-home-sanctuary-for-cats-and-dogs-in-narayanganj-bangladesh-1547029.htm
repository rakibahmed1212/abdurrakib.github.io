Can you imagine a two-storied building in Narayanganj has been turned into a shelter home for stray cats and dogs.

Dipanwita Ridi, a young and passionate animal lover, took an initiative to run the shelter away from the heart of the city to protect them from cruelty. 

As she grew up her affection towards pets became stronger and she chose to dedicate her life by fostering pet animals.
